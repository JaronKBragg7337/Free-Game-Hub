## What is Sentinel?

Sentinel is a grid-based tower defense game where you strategically place towers to stop waves of enemies from reaching the exit. Each tower type has unique strengths, and careful positioning is key to surviving the escalating onslaught.

## Why This Game?

Tower defense is a timeless genre that rewards planning, adaptation, and split-second decisions. Sentinel distills the essence into a clean, fast-playing experience that works on any device.

## Controls

**Desktop**
- Click tower button → click grid cell to place
- Click placed tower to select (shows upgrade/sell options)
- Space: Send next wave
- P: Pause

**Mobile / Touch**
- Tap tower button → tap grid cell to place
- Tap placed tower to select
- Large "NEXT WAVE" button for sending waves

## Towers

1. **Pulse** (50g) — Fast fire, low damage. Your reliable early-game defense.
2. **Cannon** (100g) — Splash damage. Shreds groups of enemies.
3. **Sniper** (150g) — Long range, high damage. Picks off the tough ones.
4. **Frost** (75g) — Slows enemies. Essential crowd control.

Upgrades: Each tower can be upgraded twice (Level 3 max). Click a tower to upgrade.

## Enemies

- **Drone** — Fast, fragile. Comes in swarms.
- **Tank** — Slow, heavily armored. Requires focused fire.
- **Swarm** — Very fast, very weak. Flood lanes.
- **Boss** — Massive HP. Appears every 10 waves.
